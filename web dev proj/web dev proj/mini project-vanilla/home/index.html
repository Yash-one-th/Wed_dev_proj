Pather Panchali(1955) Bangla

8.4

Impoverished priest Harihar Ray, dreaming of a better life for himself and his family, leaves his rural Bengal village in search of work.

Director: Satyajit Ray

Cast: Karuna Banerjee, Subir Banerjee, Chunibhala Devi

Chungking Express(1994) Chinese

8.1

Two melancholy Hong Kong policemen fall in love: one with a mysterious female underworld figure, the other with a beautiful and ethereal waitress at a late-night restaurant he frequents.

Director: Kar-Wai Wong

Cast: Brigitte Lin Takeshi Kaneshiro Tony Leung

Amélie(2001) French

8.3

Amélie is an innocent and naive girl in Paris with her own sense of justice. She decides to help those around her and, along the way, discovers love.

Director: Jean-Pierre Jeunet

Cast: Audrey Tautou, Mathieu Kassovitz, Rufus

Memories of Murder(2003) Korean

8.1

In a small Korean province in 1986, two detectives struggle with the case of multiple young women being found raped and murdered by an unknown culprit.

Director: Bong Joon-Ho

Cast: Song Kang-Ho, Kim Sang-Kyung, Kim Roi-Ha

A Seperation(2011) Persian

8.3

A married couple are faced with a difficult decision - to improve the life of their child by moving to another country or to stay in Iran and look after a deteriorating parent who has Alzheimer's disease.

Director: Asghar Farhadi

Cast: Leila Hatami, Payman Maadi, Shahab Hosseini

Malena(2000) Italian

7.5

Amidst the war climate, a teenage boy discovering himself becomes love-stricken by Malèna, a sensual woman living in a small, narrow-minded Italian town.

Director: Giuseppe Tornatore

Cast: Monica Bellucci, Giuseppe Sulfaro

City of God(2002) Portuguese

8.6

In the slums of Rio, two kids' paths diverge as one struggles to become a photographer and the other a kingpin.

Director:Fernando Meirelles, Katia Lund

Cast: Alexandre Rodrigues, Leandro Firmino, Jonathan Haagensen

Motorcycle Diaries(2004) Espanol

7.8

The dramatization of a motorcycle road trip Che Guevara went on in his youth that showed him his life's calling.

Director: Walter Salles

Cast: Gael Garcia Bernal, Rodrigo de la Serna, Mía Maestro

Solaris(1972) Russian

8.0

A psychologist is sent to a station orbiting a distant planet in order to discover what has caused the crew to go insane.

Director: Andrei Tarkovsky

Cast: Donatas Banionis, Natalya Bondarchuk, Jüri Järvet

Taxi Driver(1976) English

8.2

A mentally unstable veteran works as a nighttime taxi driver in New York City, where the perceived decadence and sleaze fuels his urge for violent action.

Director: Martin Scorsese

Cast: Robert De Niro, Jodie Foster, Albert Brooks

Satya(1998) Telugu, Hindi

8.3

An innocent man is embroiled in underworld after being falsely charged and sets out to punish the perpetrators who subjected his life to misery.

Director: Ram Gopal Varma

Cast: J D Chakravarthy, Urmila Matondkar, Paresh Rawal
